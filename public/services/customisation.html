Customisation

Is there just something missing from your instrument that you feel is holding back your style? At 5th frets we love to help you modify your instrument so you can have the best experience playing possible. We offer a wide range of services, including:

  • road-worn
  • refinishing the neck lacquer (with different lacquer or oils)
  • electronic part upgrade (see Electronic Modification)
  • add a Floyd Rose, Bigsby or other vibrato system
  • routing instrument body for new pots/switches
For further details or to make an inquiry , please feel free to contact us at any time.
