Bodywork

We provide a range of services dealing with bodywork issues, including:
  • scratch, dent or chip removal
  • fretboard damage
  • broken headstocks
  • neck resets
  • lifting bridges
  • rusted parts
  • general damage
With a wealth of experience, we work carefully and skilfully to bring your guitar back to its original glory. For further information, or for inquiries about services not listed here, please contact us.
