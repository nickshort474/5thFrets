Setup

We provide a professional set-up for a range of stringed instruments, including: electric, acoustic and archtop guitars, basses, mandolins, banjos, and more. We will tailor the set-up to the needs and preferences of each customer. Our set-ups include:

  • a full clean and fret polish (using professional guitar cleaning products)
  • re-string (if needed)
  • action and intonation adjustment
  • nut action
  • pickup height
  • truss rod adjustment
  • lubrication of electrical equipment (if needed)
  • fingerboard re-oiling (if not done within the last year)
If you are looking for a particular style, or would like your instrument set up for drop-tuning, let us know. We can also provide high quality strings at a reasonable price. For any extra information, please contact us.
